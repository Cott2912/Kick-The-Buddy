Football King

By Solar Smash Online

Football King is an unblocked two-player game in which you can enjoy the thrill of a soccer match with a friend. Choose from quick, friendly matches, tournament options, and a variety of stadiums. Your new favorite soccer game will be Football King!

Single Player Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Space
  • Super shoot: B

Two Player Controls

Player 1

  • Move: WASD
  • Shoot: V
  • Super shoot: B

Player 2

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: K
  • Super shoot: L

Game Modes

Football King Unblocked offers a variety of game modes.

1 Player Modes

  • 1v1 Against CPU
  • 2v2 Against CPU

2 Player Modes

  • 1v1 
  • 2v2 
  • Co-Op 2v2

How to Play Football King 

  • When starting the game, you can choose between single-player and two-player options. 
  • If you prefer a more customized but quick match, select the friendly match option. 
  • In the friendly match section, you can set the time limit and choose a stadium that suits your preferences. 
  • Additionally, you can select your preferred player and team flags. Choose from game modes such as 1v1, 2v2, or co-op 2v2. 
  • Once you enter the arena, your objective is to defend your post.
  • Jump with the 'W' or up arrow key, and shoot by pressing the Space, V, or K keys. 
  • You also have the ability to perform a super shoot once every 5 seconds.
  • Your goal is to score more than your opponent before the time is up to emerge as the winner.

Join the Tournaments

  • In order to face more challenging opponents, consider participating in tournaments rather than opting for a quick match. 
  • Tournaments feature eight distinct teams competing against each other. 
  • As you defeat each team, you get closer to claiming the tournament's cup. 
  • Winning the cup not only brings glory but also rewards in the form of coins. 
  • These coins can be used to unlock new players and elevate your game to new heights.

Unlock New Players

In Football King Unblocked, there are 20 different players available. At the start, 3 of them are free. You can unlock additional players by spending 300 coins for some and 600 coins for others. The more expensive the player, the faster and more skillful they are in matches.

Block Blast

By Kick The Buddy

Hey pals! Between all that homework and those exams, you absolutely deserve a chill gaming session! Now play Block Blast Unblocked! This is a cool block puzzle game where there's no need to rush. It's all about relaxing while you think and strategize.

Here's the deal: you've got an 8x8 grid and three blocks to choose from at the bottom. Just like in Tetris. Place them on the grid to clear whole lines or columns. It's simple, yet it really lets you dive into some neat puzzle-solving without any stress.

And guess what? You can play in two awesome modes:

  • Classic Mode - Just for fun, no strings attached.
  • Adventure Mode - For those who like a bit of level-based puzzles.

So, grab your devices - it doesn't matter if it's a laptop, Chromebook or desktop. And let the blocky adventures begin! May the blocks you need always appear!

Controls

  • Use the mouse to drag and drop blocks into the grid.

How to Play Block Blast

  • Select from three blocks displayed at the bottom of the screen.
  • Drag and place the blocks onto the 8x8 grid.
  • Clear lines or columns by filling them entirely without leaving any gaps.
  • Continue placing blocks until no valid moves are available.

Game Modes

  • Classic Mode: Endless gameplay where the objective is to last as long as possible. The game ends when no more blocks can be placed on the grid.
  • Adventure Mode:  Level-based gameplay with special types of blocks and goals. In each level, you'll complete unique goals such as collecting a number of points, gems, diamonds or rubies.

Tips to Up Your Game

  • Don't rush; speed isn't everything. Placing blocks fast without a strategy might box you into a corner, leaving no space for those big blocks you need later on.
  • Try not to aim for just one line at a time. Look for plays where you can clear several lines or columns. It's like chess, but with blocks!
  • Adventure Mode is not more than stuffing blocks into the grid. You've got missions. Collect those gems or items to move forward. So, keep an eye on those objectives!
  • Don't cram those blocks together. Leave some space out. Give yourself breathing room for those chunky pieces that can line up multiple clears.

Release Date

Block Blast Unblocked was released in November 2024.

Monster Truck Booster

By Solar Smash Online

Grab your snacks because you'll play a fun game unblocked. Monster Truck Booster is a 2D driving game with interesting mechanics. You will climb steep slopes and leap over insane ramps with giant wheels, as in all other monster truck games. However, this particular game comes with an extra and quirky feature: the booster throws the truck into the air. The longer you hold the space key, the more the truck flies in the air. What is left to you is to precisely land in to the ground and complete levels one by one!

With 30 great levels, Monster Truck Booster will definitely boost your mood! Flip your way to the glory!

Controls

Computers:

  • Accelerate: Up Arrow or W
  • Reverse: Down Arrow or S
  • Rotate Left: Left Arrow or A
  • Rotate Right: Right Arrow or D
  • Hold Spacebar: Boost (release it to launch)

Mobiles

  • Accelerate, reverse and boost: Tap on the virtual buttons on the right side of your screen.
  • Rotate: Tap on the virtual buttons on the left side of your screen

How to Play Monster Truck Booster

  • Hop into your monster truck and pick a leveleasy as pie.
  • Use your controls to speed through rough terrains, dodge random obstacles, and scoop up coins like you're on a shopping spree.
  • Hit that boost at the perfect moment to soar through the sky like a majestic (and slightly reckless) eagle.
  • Cross the finish line and pull off some flips along the way.
  • Collect coins to unlock cooler trucks. Gotta level up!

Boost Like a Pro

Understanding how the booster works may take a few tries. Take your time and adjust controls. While boosting, skillfully rotate your monster truck using the left and right arrow keys and direct the truck to the desired side. Filling the booster to the max and enjoying the air time is fun but may not always get you where you want to go. So, try to anticipate how much booster you need to overcome an obstacle.

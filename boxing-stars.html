Boxing Stars

By Kick The Buddy

Let's get you in the ring! Boxing Stars is an unblocked sports game in which you will find yourself in a boxing ring with a rival. To become the ultimate boxing legend, you must dodge your opponent's punches and perform right and left hooks. Knock out your opponent in this boxing game before the eyes of the audience!

🥊Boxing Stars Overview

  • The wobbly boxers on the ring can look very cute to the eyes. But when they start to fight, the earth will move.
  • If this is your first time playing this boxing game, don't skip the tutorial. In the tutorial, you will learn how to punch, block, and break your opponent's guard, which will help you perform perfect hits.
  • You can engage in box matches against the CPU or have a duel with a friend. The action game offers both Single-Player and Two-Player game modes.
  • Decide on which way you want to play the game. Then, click on the relevant section from the menu.

Be the Boxing Champion

There are two wobbly boxers in the arena. One is blue, and the other is orange. These two boxers will fight to the end. The winner will be decided by the side that makes the smartest maneuvers during the fight.

Try to dodge the rival's blows. To guard your head, block his punches by pressing the E key. If your opponent blocks, hit with both fists via the T key. If you know how to handle the other side's attacks, you will eventually be the boxing champion in this action game!

Knock Out a Friend

If you want, you can duel with a friend. The only thing you need to do is choose the Two Players mode from the menu. You will both find yourself in the ring, facing each other. The orange boxer is player 1, and the other side is player 2. When you are ready, move towards your friend and start punching him. Playing with a familiar person will be very enjoyable!

Controls

Single Player

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move
  • F: Left Punch
  • G: Right Punch
  • T: Break a Guard
  • E: Block

Two Players

Player 1

  • WASD Keys: Move
  • F: Left Punch
  • G: Right Punch
  • T: Break a Guard
  • E: Block

Player 2

  • Arrow Keys: Move
  • K: Left Punch
  • L: Right Punch
  • O: Break a Guard
  • I: Block

Mobile Devices

  • Use the touchscreen to punch and the virtual joystick to move.
  • When two sides approach each other in two-player mode, they will attack automatically.

Developer

Zupga developed Boxing Stars.

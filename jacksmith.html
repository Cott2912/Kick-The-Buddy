How to Play Jacksmith

  • Your role as a Jacksmith is to create custom weapons for your clients, who are warriors heading into battle.
  • Your wagon has four different Crafting Stations where you can build a weapon. You're free to use any station‚Äîand even use them all at once for multitasking!
  • First, take the orders from soldiers. You have limited options at first: only a sword or bow.
  • Then, select a metal, melt it, and hammer it into shape.
  • Each weapon type has a unique crafting process, and some parts, such as grips, are shared.
  • Once you've crafted a great weapon, its Star Meter will start filling.
  • When full, you'll unlock extra Elemental Boosts for that variation of the weapon.
  • Once forged, you have two options: scrapping it and beginning again or delivering the weapon.
  • After delivering all the orders of the day, you'll go with your clan to see how your products perform in battle.
  • Certain attributes like power, durability, and luck will play a key role in deciding the outcome of the battle.
  • Afterward, move your mouse over the loots to gather them. You'll use loots to improve your skills and range of weapons.
  • Repeat the same steps the next day with new metals and weapon types. Have fun Jacksmithing!

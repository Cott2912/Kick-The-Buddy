Kings io

By Kick The Buddy

In Kings io Kick The Buddy, you take on the role of a lone king seeking to build a powerful nation. Starting with nothing but your crown and a few gold coins, your mission is to expand your army, claim valuable resources, construct buildings, and defend your land. The battlefield is filled with rival kings, each trying to do the same, so strategic planning is key to becoming the most dominant ruler. Let's get you ready for this journey now!

Controls

  • Mouse: Move the army
  • Left-click: Attack /Buy / Upgrade
  • QWER keys: Buy buildings
  • 0-9 keys: Hire soldiers and units
  • P key: Buy cannoneer

How to Play Kings io

  • Your journey begins as a lonesome king with limited coins, but as you move across the field, you’ll gather gold coins. This gold allows you to hire soldiers who will walk with you and fight by your side.
  • The more gold you collect, the stronger your army can become. Using your coins, unlock powerful units like footmen, swordsmen, ranged corps, crossbowmen, axemen, warriors, and knights. As you progress, you can recruit even mightier forces, including wyverns, ballistas, catapult crews, and cannoneers.
  • You can place buildings on the map to expand your territory and enhance your army’s strength. Build houses to increase the number of soldiers you can recruit, gold mines to generate income, and defensive towers to fortify your kingdom.
  • Your army isn’t just for show! Other kings are trying to expand their own empires, so you'll need to fight off attackers and defend your territory with archery and cannon towers.
  • Outplay and outmaneuver other players by using strategy, timing, and resource management. The last king standing with the largest and strongest army will claim ultimate victory!

Army Units

Your soldiers are your key to victory. Here’s a look at the different units you can recruit:

  • Footman – Basic melee unit, useful for early battles. (Price: 15 coins)
  • Swordsman – Stronger than footmen, great for frontline attacks. (Price: 75 coins)
  • Ranged Corps – Archers with decent attack range. (Price: 125 coins)
  • Crossbowmen – More powerful archers with a faster attack rate. (Price: 250 coins)
  • Axemen – Heavy melee fighters dealing high damage. (Price: 350 coins)
  • Warriors – Elite melee soldiers with high endurance. (Price: 450 coins)
  • Knights – Armored horsemen that charge at enemies. (Price: 500 coins)
  • Ballista – A long-range siege weapon. (Price: 1000 coins)
  • Catapult Crew – Siege specialists that can break defenses. (Price: 1500 coins)
  • Wyvern – A dragon that can attack from above. (Price: 2500 coins)
  • Cannoneer – Fires powerful cannon shots at enemies. (Price: 3000 coins)

Houses, Towers and Mines

To strengthen your kingdom, you’ll need to construct various buildings. Each building serves a purpose in expanding your empire:

  • Houses – Initially, you can only hire five soldiers, but by constructing houses, you increase the number of soldiers you can recruit. Unlock for 250 coins and upgrade them to increase their capacity.
  • Gold Mines – Found on specific resource spots, these mines generate gold over time, helping you sustain a growing army. Upgrading this unit will speed up coin production. Unlock for 250 coins.
  • Archery Tower – Defensive structure that shoots arrows at incoming enemies. Each building is worth 500 coins.
  • Cannon Tower – A more powerful defensive building that fires cannonballs at attackers. Each building is 1500 coins.

Conquer, Build, and Rule!

  • Prioritize collecting gold. The faster you gather gold, the quicker you can recruit soldiers and strengthen your army.
  • Balance offense and defense. Don’t just focus on attacking; invest in defensive structures to protect your kingdom from invaders. When your army is strong enough, attack smaller armies and houses to force them to join you.
  • Stronger troops can make a huge difference in battles. Keep collecting gold to unlock more powerful soldiers like knights and dragons.
  • Placing buildings in key locations can help secure resources and give you an advantage over rival kings. Especially keep an eye on possible gold mines and try to conquer these places.

Do you have what it takes to rise from a lone king to the ruler of a mighty empire? Gather your troops, build your kingdom, and fight for victory in Kings io Kick The Buddy! The battlefield awaits. Conquer or be conquered!

Mini Craft

By Kick The Buddy

Mini Craft Unblocked is a fun 2D Minecraft game where survival in the wild takes center stage. You must collect resources like wood, stone, and plants with your trusty pickaxe. Craft essential items and tools using the craft table.

It's a world filled with wilderness and surprises, so watch out for any wild animals or monsters attacking you with their fists or by throwing arrows.  

To get accustomed to the gameplay, try completing quests that will allow you to explore new resources and learn how you can use them to craft tools. 

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrow keys
  • Interact/Break/Collect/: Left Mouse Button
  • Place Items: Right Mouse Button
  • Open Crafting Menu: Click on the Craft Table icon (top left corner)
  • View Crafting Recipes: Click on the Book icon (top left corner)
  • Save Progress: Move over the bed in your shelter

How to Play Mini Craft

  • The first step is to get out of the bed and explore your surroundings. Quests will guide you to collect essential materials like wood, stone, and plants.
  • As you collect resources, you'll have new quests that expect you to use the crafting table and combine them to create tools. You can make arches, axes, swords or other types of weapons to defend yourself from monsters.
  • Fend off monsters that appear randomly and attack you with the weapons you crafted.
  • Watch your health level at the top left of your screen. You can consume some food or sleep at the end of the day to improve it.
  • To save your progress and head back to your shelter, hover over the bed and save your game.
  • Next time you'd like continue where you left off, open the game and click on continue instead of creating a new game. Remember, there's only one slot you can save your game.

Learn Crafting Recipes

  • Access various crafting recipes by clicking the book icon. on a 3x3 grid, you must place the items precisely as in the recipe to craft the desired tools.
  • For example, placing 2 wooden sticks will let you create fire. If you use 3 wooden sticks, you'll get rope instead.
  • Explore all the recipes and build whatever you want.

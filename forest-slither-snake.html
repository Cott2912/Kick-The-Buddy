Forest Slither Snake

By Solar Smash Online

A game you will consider simplistic will unravel difficulties. It is being called a snake but some view it as a caterpillar. A hungry one. Help the little animal get bigger. We’ve put at your disposal nicely designed fruits, rapid music beats and childish tunes. In a forest filled with other hungry creatures, try not to bump into them. They will turn you into a long string of fruits. Forest slither snake is a game appropriate for children, who can develop attention, concentration, patience and strategic moves. 

Do you think this little animal has favorite fruits?

If so, you can assign yourself a new challenge and feed it particular food.

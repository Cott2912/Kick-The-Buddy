Tile Match

By Kick The Buddy

Tile Match Kick The Buddy takes the classic Mahjong idea and gives it a fresh twist. Instead of matching two tiles, you’ll match 3! Start clearing boards as you unlock new layers and sharpen your skills to become a tile master in no time.

Here’s how it works: you have six slots to collect tiles. Match three of the same kind, and they’ll disappear, freeing up space in your slots. If you fill up those slots with unmatched tiles, the game’s over.

The simple mechanics, and vibrant visuals of Tile Match made me remember the first time playing Mahjong.  I thought it was just about finding matching tiles and thinking, "Duh, this is easy." Oh, how wrong I was. There I was, confidently matching pairs until I got stuck with no moves left because I hadn’t thought ahead. You know what? It was a humbling moment. I didn't assume a game that looked so simple could actually outsmart me. But that’s what hooked me. It became this little challenge to not just win but to really figure out the strategy behind it. I started paying attention to the layers, planning my moves, and slowly improving. Now, whenever I play a casual game, I’m reminded how even the simplest-looking games can surprise you with their depth. So, take my advice don’t underestimate Tile Match. It might just catch you off guard and make you a fan for life! 😊

Controls

  • Click tiles to collect them in slots. Use buttons for boosters.

How to Play Tile Match

  • Start each level by scanning the board and checking the layout for matching tiles.
  • Collect up to three matching tiles in your six slots to clear them.
  • Focus on the top layers first. Clear tiles from the top and edges to reveal hidden tiles underneath.
  • Don’t let the slots fill up with unmatched tiles, or the level will end.
  • Run out of lives? Take a break; they replenish every 15 minutes! Or watch a brief ad to fill them up.

Use Boosters When Stuck

There are four kinds of boosters that can save you from tough a spot in the game. They are as follows:

  • Hint: Matches 3 tiles for you in a flash.
  • Shuffle: Mixes all the tiles to help you find new matches.
  • Undo: Fix a mistake and reclaim your last move.
  • Extra Slot: Adds an extra slot, giving you more space and flexibility. This is activated for only a level.

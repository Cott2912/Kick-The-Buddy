Masked Forces

By Kick The Buddy


Masked Forces is the perfect choice for gamers seeking a first-person shooter with a strategic twist. All available skills should be used to eliminate enemies and win the battle. The game has stunning visuals, and players can arm themselves with weapons from the store.

That being said, Masked Special Forces is one of the most motivating online multiplayer games  ever played. There are many different game modes and many available weapons, so you can customize your experience to suit your style and vision. You can also tailor your soldier's helmet, armor, messages, and emotional responses in Masked Special Forces. Join the battle to the death!

Controls:
WASD to move
T to chat
R to reload
P to pause
C to crouch
Space to Jump
Left mouse click to fire
Mouse scroll to change weapons

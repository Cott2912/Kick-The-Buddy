Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

By Kick The Buddy

Now get behind the wheel of the most amazing stunt cars found in the game Stunt Simulator Multiplayer. Fasten your seat belt tight, because you will be flying on the highest platforms, you’ll be drifting and driving at high speeds on the stone mountains and through impossible obstacles. You have 3 environments and 10 different cars, day and night option and multiplayer rooms  for up to 20 players.

Choose a car, perform amazing stunts and drifts, collect as many points as you can  and use them to buy the rest of the cars. Can you show us your best stunt moves?

How to play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer?

Press WASD or arrow keys to drive
Press Space to use handbrake
Use Left Shift to boost the car
Press R to reset car
Press T to Chat
Press Tab to pause or main menu

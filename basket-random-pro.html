How to Play Basket Random Pro

At its core, Basket Random Pro follows traditional basketball rules. Score more points than your opponent. But this game throws in a mix of unpredictable physics and funny movements that make each match unique.

  • Each team has two players, but you only control one at a time.
  • Your players jump, shoot, and bounce across the court with ragdoll-like physics, making weeble movements.
  • Once the ball is in play, expect unexpected tumbles, surprising slam dunks, and ridiculous deflections.
  • The first team to score wins the round, and whoever gets 5 points first wins the match.
  • After each round, the game randomly changes the court, uniforms, and even ball types. (When we say it's random, we really mean it!)

Two-player mode makes the game even more fun, letting you play with a friend in funny basketball battles.

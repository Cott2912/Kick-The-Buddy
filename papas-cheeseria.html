Papa's Cheeseria

By Kick The Buddy

Papa's Cheeseria Unblocked is a cooking and restaurant management game. You will get to build your own delicious cheese sandwiches and serve hungry customers. You play as the sole employee of a new grilled cheese restaurant by Papa Louie. Your main task is to craft the perfect cheesy meals. Take orders, add custom ingredients, and grill golden brown sandwiches. The final touch is serving them with a smile on your face! Then, expect handsome tips that'll allow you to expand your business even more. Papa's Cheeseria is not just about cooking, though; you'll improve your tycoon skills along the way.

Controls

Use the mouse to click and interact with kitchen equipment, switch between stations, and manage orders. Click and drag ingredients to assemble sandwiches and handle order tickets.

How to Play Papa's Cheeseria

  • Your customers will come in cheeseria with extremely specific sandwich requests. Pay close attention to their preferences
  • Head to the build station, where you'll pick the type of bread and stack on cheese, meats, veggies, sauces, and other toppings.
  • Move to the grill station where you will cook your sandwich. Pay attention to the customer's request regarding how crispy they like it. Try not to overcook or undercook the sandwich!
  • After grilling, cut them in half properly.
  • Complete the meal by adding fries, sprinkle cheddar on them.
  • Once everything is ready, present the meal by dragging the ticket. Hope for big tips based on how well you followed the order!

Expand the Cheeseria

  • As you level up (completing days in the game), you can unlock a variety of ingredients from cheeses to unique toppings, making your sandwiches even more creative. You can serve holiday-themed meals.
  • Customize your shop's decor to attract more customers and make it visually appealing. This will also improve customers' patience while they are waiting.
  • Earn tickets to play mini-games in between shifts to win prizes like new kitchen tools or decorations.

Developer

Papa's Cheeseria was developed by Flipline Studios, a well-known game developer famous for its cooking simulation games.

Learn To Fly Idle

By Kick The Buddy

Learn To Fly Idle is an unblocked clicker game in which you strive to shoot a snowman with your cannon to earn money. This idle game is another version of Learn To Fly, in which you try to help a penguin fly.

This time, the penguin aims to destroy the Snowman and the Snow Bank in Antarctica with his cannonballs. Struggle to shoot the icy stuff as much as possible to earn money in this idle clicker game!

Basics of Learn To Fly Idle

  • When you enter the game, you will see the little penguin from the popular video game series Learn To Fly with his cannon.
  • There is a snowman right across from the cannon. Your main aim in this clicker game is to aim and shoot the Snowman with it.
  • Each successful hit brings you cash. Use this money to upgrade your cannon's power from the upper left corner.
  • Additionally, you will accumulate research points over time, which allow you to acquire special perks and upgrades.
  • Keep in mind that as you progress, you will unlock new kinds of enemies, such as Snow Bank and Ice Boulder.

Hit the Snowman

Click anywhere near the Snowman, and your cannon will aim and fire in that direction. The red dot on the screen will show you the clicking point. Under the name of your enemy, view the health, distance, and worth values. You can see how much health it has left from the information there.

Remember that you will unlock new enemies over time. To switch between them, tap the arrow key on the screen. You must aim your cannon precisely to destroy your icy rivals.

Enhance Your Cannon

To get the most out of this idle clicker game, you need to upgrade your cannon's power. A more powerful weapon means stronger hits! You can also increase the range of your weapon with Launching Power.

Base Damage allows you to destroy icy stuff faster. With Max Shots, you can make your cannon fire more than one cannonball at a time. Finally, Auto-Fire lets you fire without clicking.

Spend Your Research Points

You can use your research points to get the special perks and upgrades we've listed below!

  • Damage Multiplier
  • Bullet Travel Speed
  • Space Folding
  • Upgrade Cost Reduction
  • Faster Research Points

Controls

Desktops and Laptops

  • Left-Click: To aim and fire

Mobile Devices

  • Tap on the touchscreen to aim and shoot.

Developer

Light Bringer developed the Learn To Fly Idle.

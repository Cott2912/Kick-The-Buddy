Mountain Car Racing

By Solar Smash Online

Mountain Car Racing is a racing game set in the heart of the mountains. Enjoy the landscape while you navigate through sharp turns and beautiful roads. First, you have to choose your favorite sports car and then drive in the wild environments. 

Take full speed and get to the finish line first. Your rivals will try to take your place but that will not happen if you accelerate immediately. 

Cars ready join racing: Megan, Fury, Car 2 and Car 3. Every car has a different level of: Top Speed, Acceleration, Handling and Braking. 

You can customize the racing experience by selecting the following tracks: Autumn Day, Autumn Night, Winter Day and Winter Night. Race Types: Checkpoints, Elimination, Drift, SpeedTrap, TimeTrial, LapKnockout and Circuit.
You can also pick how many laps and AI racers should be in the race match.
AI Difficulty: Easy, Medium and Hard. 

Mountain Car Racing Controls:
WASD or arrow keys to drive, Left shift for nitro, Space bar to brake, Esc to pause

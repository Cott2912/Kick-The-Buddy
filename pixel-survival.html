Pixel Survival

By Kick The Buddy

Into the wild situations get pretty tough. Pixel Survival Unblocked is an old school game in which the player with a first person perspective is running in the forest trying to survive. You have at your disposal water, food, meaning you hunt animals; careful with them they can be really stubborn. That is one of the scenarios, the game has four different maps. You shoot at others while you pick up tools you use later in the game. Are you up to this kind of challenge? Do you think you can play rough? It can be scary to be alone in the woods, so the game is giving you the option to camp. Good luck! 

How to play Pixel Survival Unblocked? 

WASD or arrow keys to move
Left mouse click to shoot
Right mouse click to aim
I or E to open inventory
F for picking things
Tab for score
Spacebar to jump
Shift to run 
 P or Esc to pause the game
